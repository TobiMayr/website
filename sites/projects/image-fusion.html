Image Fusion

Bachelor thesis title: "Conception and Development of an Android Game based on predicting image overlays"

Hardware: Android Smartphone

Software: Unity (C#)

I wrote my bachelor thesis at the HTW-Berlin in 2018 about a game I developed called "Image Fusion".
The player has to find the right combination of images in order to recreate a predetermined image. This project was realised with C# in Unity, it is publicised on the Play Store. Throughout the last phase of development I deployed the app as an open beta in the Play Store to get feedback. This has helped a great deal to identify and prioritise issues which then could be resolved.
