Leap Hockey

A desktop Air Hockey game which can be controlled with hand gestures that a recorded with the Leap Motion Controller

Hardware: Leap Motion Controller

Software: Unity (C#)

One course at HTW-Berlin called "Development of Multimedia Systems" tasked me in a group of three to create an Application that experiments with newer devices such as the leap motion controller.
We chose to create a Air Hockey game that can be controlled through hand gestures. More details can be read in our report:
